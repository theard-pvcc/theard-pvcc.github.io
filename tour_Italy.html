Holiday Roma

Visit Rome with us! Holiday Roma Tours are the most exciting way to visit this ancient Italian city. Stay in the luxurious Hotel Farnese, enjoy fabulous meals each day, and visit Rome's most renown sites and museums on our Hop On/ Hop Off buses. Your trip will also include a night of musical theater at the Teatro Sistina!
